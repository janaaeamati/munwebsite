Position Paper Requirements

Header:

  • Committee Language+Name (ex. "French General Assembly")
  • Committee Topic
  • Optional: Country Flag

  • DO NOT INCLUDE ANY IDENTIFYING INFORMATION IN THE HEADING OR BODY OF PAPER (ex. school, name, etc.)

Formatting:

  • One-Inch Margins
  • 1 or 1.15 Spacing
  • Times New Roman, Size 10-12

Citations:

  • MLA, APA, or Chicago with proper formatting for in-text citations
  • Works Cited/References Page MUST be included with proper formatting

DISCLAIMER: COMMITTEE CHAIRS HAVE BEEN GIVEN THE DESCRETION TO USE AN AI DETECTOR ON ALL PAPERS SUBMITTED. THOSE FOUND GUILTY OF USING AI WILL NOT BE ELIGIBLE FOR AWARDS ON CONFERENCE DAY.
